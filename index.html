Copernicus' Cradle: A Relaxation Ritual

Take a moment to rest your mind by engaging in this short, relaxing ritual.

Instructions:

  1. When you are feeling drained and your productivity is suffering, take a break from your task.
  2. Prepare yourself for the ritual. Get a glass of tea, a cup of water, or whatever else you may want to accompany you. This helps to separate the mind from the previous task.
  3. Begin play on the minigame below. Place orbs by pressing the space bar and remove them by pressing backspace. Create fun an interesting patterns with the movements of the orbs. Change the speed of the orbs by adjusting the slider.
  4. Fixate on the hypnotic patterns you create, allowing your mind to rest and recharge.
  5. When you are feeling recharged, exit the ritual and return to the task at hand.
